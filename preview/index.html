G9 logo — Thrust × G

The chosen direction is concept-03 “Thrust”. The brief: “the Thrust with some proper G in/with the horizontal lines (weight them down).” Below are 8 explorations that integrate a legible G with the 9 (so the mark reads G9) and vary how the horizontal motion lines belong to the system — and how heavy they are. Each is shown large on a light card and on the locked Rose Peach background, plus favicon sizes (24 / 32 px). Nothing is picked yet — dial in the feel. The original 12 concepts remain below for reference. Marks are monochrome SVG using currentColor; files live in public/brand/logo-concepts/thrust/.
